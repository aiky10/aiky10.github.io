About Me

I am an aspiring multidisciplinary designer with a BFA in Industrial Design from the University of the Philippines-Diliman. I am is also a UPJMA alumnus where I served in the internal and special projects committee. During my academic years, I was consistently praised as well-rounded and very creative by my professors and peers. I developed skills in product conceptualization, marketing & promotions, being hands-on with a team, and creating experiences with the end-user in mind by integrating my extracurricular and academic activities. I consider myself an advocate of thoughtful, sustainable, inspiring, and human-centered design through efficient design processes. I am collaborative, critical, and hungry to learn and improve how humans interact with digital and physical products as well as push visual communication to different spaces in the Philippines.

For me, living a balanced lifestyle is the key to a fulfilling life. Especially in the digital age where everything is within our reach, we must learn to strike a balance between consumption and creation; or at the very least convert the experience of consuming something into something productive.

I have many hobbies such as baking bread, watching anime and reading manga, drawing, playing videogames, basketball & occasionally the drums. I am deeply intrigued by the subject of design, manufacturing/DIY products, engineering, carpentry, science, emerging technologies, animation, Youtube & Twitch, movie & music production, cars & motorcycles, and the k-pop industry. I love to delve into anything audio-visual.
