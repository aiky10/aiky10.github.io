The Orbit watchface is a concept watch face initially designed for one my classses in college. It's directly inspired by our solar system where plants orbit the sun. Instead of having hands that tell that time, I used nodes that will orbit the center of the watch's axis. The outer ring represent the hour similar to how the hour hand is the longest hand and therefore points the farthest. The middle ring serves as the minute hand and finally the innermost rings serves as the second hand since it will be constantly moving; I assigned the smallest orbit to it. I chose black for the colors to represent the deep abyss of space. The time indicators are all gold plated to represent the stars in the galaxy. I decided to separate them instead of one ring for better recognition instead of put. The nodes are black outside for uniformity and white on the inside for visibility. They also glow in the dark like a common watch. Fusion 360 was used to design and render this project.s
