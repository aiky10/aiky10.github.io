The Spotify app has taken the music world by storm. It's a dominantly social & reliant on the internet music app that requires a subscription. With Audra, I wanted to attempt in developing something more similar to iTunes where you can simply upload music files that you already have and view album art while also integrating some social and online aspects to it. Audra acts as go-to app for anything audio as it also includes a voice recorder & sampler for musicians on the go.

There are features to this app that are inspired by Spotify & iTunes. I wanted to try marrying these two while also creating something different. In player mode, I wanted to keep the search bar at the top at all times for easy access and queueing of songs. Beside the album cover art, I included an equalizer graphic animation. The scrubber is also elongated vertically so for more accurate finger placement and scrubbing. The play/pause/previous/next buttons are used as divider between the upper graphics and the list of songs comming up. The search feature allows for previously searched songs to viewd and also a voice search similar to Shazam. Audra can also be used to store and listen to podcasts
