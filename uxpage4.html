Trakk is desktop application for organizing and tracking all kinds of files & downloads. Other than being a hub, there is a social aspect to this application where community members can upload files and review those files if they're clean or not through comments, reports and review write-ups.

The application opens with your recent downloads & recently finished downloads that you want to keep track of. There's also a quick view sectiong for your current downloads and updates on community uploads. All of these also lead the user to the full view of these features. The library primarly shows the files in list form to show as much information on each file that you have on your drive. A large section on the right moves you to specific catergories of files that you want to access. The download queue is patterned after torrent clients. Colors for the progress bars & playback functions are assigned for easier recognition. The Community feature takes after shopping apps where theres an image/cover of what you want to download and is followed by details that are relevant to the user. Below the information is where users can read comments on the different sources that are supplied by the app.
