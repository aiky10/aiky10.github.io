The objective for this project was to create a landing, log-in and registration page for a website. At the time I made this, Adidas' website had an outdated look and very clunky registration on their website. I updated it by including new photos from their new campaign and used more graphic elements and an assymetric layouts to the lead the viewer down the page. Maintaing the brand also an apsect that I kept in mind.

For the log-in and registration. I added graphics again to fill out the page and keep users aware of what's going with the brand. Enlarged logos for logging in with 3rd party service for ease of access. I tried to keep registration as simple as possible so that the user can start right away after filling out the requirements. I also added marketing and graphic elements to entice users to register to the website.
