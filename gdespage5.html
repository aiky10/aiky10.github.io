This project was a class requirement in college. The class was divided into groups and tasked to pick a Filipino snack and design a bag for the product. My group chose chichacorn as our product. The chichacorn photos were taken by our group and we had our own individual and final designs.

My goal for the packaging was to have the chichacorn photo be the hero of the design. I tried achieving this by using a simple background color and then adding a stylized fonts to be the balance between the photo and the background. I also wanted to explore a carnival type of packaging to act as an outlier among the studies. I also made sure to try the designs on different bag types to see which fit the best not just graphically but also for the food product.
